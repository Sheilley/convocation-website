About the Event

On behalf of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC), it is with immense pleasure that I, the President of the Convocation, extend a warm welcome to all our esteemed guests, faculty, staff, and most importantly, our graduating class of 2024. On this day we gather to celebrate your remarkable achievements and the culmination of your dedicated efforts in the field of statistics. Join us for the 7th EASTC Convocation 2024 to celebrate the achievements of our graduates. Our institution continues the tradition of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community on Africa's Statistics Day.
